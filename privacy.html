Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 1, 2025

Our Commitment to Privacy

VoidCaptcha values your privacy and is committed to protecting it.

Information We Collect

VoidCaptcha is designed with privacy in mind. Unlike many other CAPTCHA solutions, we do not:

  • Collect any personally identifiable information
  • Use cookies or tracking technologies
  • Store IP addresses or device identifiers
  • Profile users or their behavior

The only information processed is whether the user successfully completed the CAPTCHA challenge, which is temporarily stored in memory and not persisted.

How We Use Information

The sole purpose of VoidCaptcha is to distinguish human users from automated bots. The verification result is used exclusively for this purpose and is not used for any other tracking or analytics.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to ensure that any temporary data processed by VoidCaptcha is protected against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at voidisopenscience@gmail.com.
